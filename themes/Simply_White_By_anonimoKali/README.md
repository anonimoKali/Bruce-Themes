The search for the perfect balance ends here. **Simply White** is a tribute to clean design and functional aesthetics. By removing borders and complex patterns, this theme provides the most streamlined experience available for **Bruce Firmware**.

It’s bold, it’s white, and it’s remarkably sharp on every screen. Whether you're in full sunlight or working in a lab, **Simply White** ensures your tools are the only thing you focus on.

### ✨ Highlights
* **True Minimalist Design:** No borders, no clutter—just high-definition white icons on a deep black background.
* **Maximum Legibility:** Large, centered typography and icons for instant recognition.
* **OLED Optimized:** Minimal pixel usage to preserve battery life while maintaining high impact.
* **Versatile Aesthetic:** Looks like professional industrial equipment on any supported hardware.

### 📱 Supported Versions (Included in ZIP):
- **105px:** M5StickC Plus / Plus 2
- **140px:** T-Embed CC1101 / Plus
- **180px:** CYD (Cheap Yellow Display) & T-Deck
- **192px:** T-LoRa-Pager

### 📦 What's Inside?
The attached `.zip` file contains:
- Organized folder structure for a seamless "Drag & Drop" installation.
- `Simply_White.json` configuration files for all device resolutions.
- High-contrast minimalist white icons and assets.

### 🚀 Installation
1. Download `Simply_White_By_anonimoKali.zip` below.
2. Extract the archive to your computer.
3. Copy the folders into the `/themes` directory on your SD card.
4. Go to **Settings > Themes** and select **Simply_White**.
