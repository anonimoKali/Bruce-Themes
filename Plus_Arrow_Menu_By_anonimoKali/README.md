This is, without a doubt, the most refined and visually stunning theme I have created for **Bruce Firmware**. **Plus Arrow Menu** is the result of countless hours of optimization to achieve the perfect tactical HUD (Heads-Up Display) experience.

Because of its superior design and balance, this theme is now the **official featured theme** of this repository.

### ✨ Why it's the Best
* **Dynamic Arrow HUD:** Unique directional arrows that frame the central icons, creating a futuristic "targeting system" look.
* **Complex Geometry:** High-detail corner accents and technical lines that maximize the aesthetic of your hardware.
* **Elite Readability:** Every pixel has been placed to ensure that icons pop with a neon-like glow against the deep black background.
* **Unified Interface:** A consistent, high-end professional feel across all menus and sub-menus.

### 📱 Supported Versions (Included in ZIP):
- **105px:** M5StickC Plus / Plus 2
- **140px:** T-Embed CC1101 / Plus (**Recommended**)
- **180px:** CYD (Cheap Yellow Display) & T-Deck
- **192px:** T-LoRa-Pager

### 📦 What's Inside?
The attached `.zip` file contains:
- Premium asset pack with the new Arrow UI elements.
- `Plus_Arrow_Menu.json` master configuration files.
- Full resolution support for the entire Bruce-compatible family.

### 🚀 Installation
1. Download `Plus_Arrow_Menu_By_anonimoKali.zip` below.
2. Extract to your PC.
3. Transfer folders to the `/themes` directory on your SD card.
4. Set it as your primary theme in **Settings > Themes**.
